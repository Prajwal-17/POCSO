Latest Legal Updates & Child Safety Cases in India

Recent POCSO Act Cases & Updates

Hosur School Abuse Case

In Hosur, Tamil Nadu, a 61-year-old private school owner, along with four others—including his wife and a teacher—was arrested under the POCSO Act for the sexual harassment of a 9-year-old girl.

Source: The Times of India

Moradabad Father Arrested

A 40-year-old man in Moradabad was booked under the POCSO Act for allegedly raping and impregnating his 15-year-old daughter. The victim informed her mother, leading to the investigation.

Source: The Times of India

Nagpur Minor Pregnant After Rape

In Nagpur, a 16-year-old girl was found to be pregnant after abdominal pain complaints. She had been sexually assaulted by a classmate of the same age. The accused has been arrested.

Source: The Times of India

Kanpur Lawyer Charged with Extortion

In Kanpur, a lawyer and his associate were charged with extorting ₹50 lakh by filing a false rape case under the POCSO Act against a BJP leader. Police confirmed the extortion claim.

Source: The Times of India

Gender-Neutral Application of POCSO

The Karnataka High Court reaffirmed that the POCSO Act is gender-neutral, holding that both men and women can be tried for child sexual assault.

Source: NDTV

Age of Consent Debate

The Supreme Court is reviewing petitions seeking to lower the age of consent from 18 to 16 years. While some advocate for change, the government opposes it to safeguard minors.

Source: LawBeat
