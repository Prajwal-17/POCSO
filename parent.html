POCSO Act Awareness Video

This video provides a comprehensive overview of the POCSO (Protection of Children from Sexual Offences) Act, one of the most important legal frameworks in India to protect children from sexual abuse, exploitation, and harassment. The video highlights the rights of children, responsibilities of parents and teachers, and the role of law enforcement agencies.

Viewers will learn to recognize early signs of abuse, understand proper reporting procedures, and discover ways to support affected children emotionally and legally. The video also emphasizes creating a safe and protective environment in schools, homes, and communities.

About Shield 360: This video is part of Shield 360, an initiative aimed at raising awareness about child safety and POCSO compliance. Shield 360 provides parents, teachers, and communities with interactive learning modules, surveys, and resources to better protect children and ensure they grow up in a safe environment. By participating in Shield 360, users can track their learning progress, attend surveys, and engage with educational content designed specifically to prevent child abuse.

Parent Roles After Watching the Video:

  • Educate children about personal safety and rights in an age-appropriate manner.
  • Maintain open communication so children feel safe reporting any uncomfortable situations.
  • Observe and recognize any unusual behavior or signs of distress in children.
  • Report any suspected abuse to the proper authorities immediately.
  • Provide emotional support and guide children through legal or counseling processes if required.
  • Use Shield 360 resources to continue learning and implementing child safety measures at home.
