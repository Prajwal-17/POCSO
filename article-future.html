Fight for Every Child. Protect Their Future

Every child deserves a future free of fear. Beyond raising awareness, Shield360 equips children with self-defence skills and confidence-building lessons that prepare them for real-life challenges.

Self-defence is not just about fighting back — it’s about building courage, awareness, and resilience in children.

Our Self-Defence Progr ams

We partner with schools, NGOs, and community centers to deliver structured programs that empower children through hands-on training and awareness workshops.

Martial Arts Basics

Karate & Taekwondo sessions that teach discipline, balance, and basic defence moves for children aged 6+.

Situational Awareness

Workshops on recognizing unsafe situations, identifying trusted adults, and saying “No” with confidence.

Cyber Safety Lessons

Protecting children from online predators, cyberbullying, and harmful digital content.

School Safety Drills

Interactive drills in schools where children practice escape plans, group safety, and peer support strategies.

"Empowered children become fearless leaders of tomorrow."

Call to Action

We encourage parents, schools, and communities to adopt self-defence and safety awareness programs. Together, we can create a safer and more resilient future for every child.
