Raise Voices. Raise Awareness

Children are the future of our nation, yet millions suffer in silence due to abuse, neglect, and exploitation. Awareness is the first step toward building a safe and supportive environment for every child.

Did you know? Every 8 minutes, a child in India faces abuse. Most cases go unreported because children feel unheard.

Why Awareness Matters

Creating awareness ensures that communities can identify, prevent, and report abuse at the earliest stages. By speaking up, we empower children and protect them from harm.

How You Can Help

  • Teach children about safe and unsafe touch.
  • Encourage open conversations at home and in schools.
  • Report suspicious activity immediately to Childline 1098.
  • Share campaigns and awareness programs in your community.
"Silence protects the abuser, not the child. Raising your voice can save a life."

Shield360’s Role

At Shield360, we run awareness drives in schools, empower parents with resources, and provide training sessions for teachers. Our mission is simple — no child should ever feel unsafe or alone.
